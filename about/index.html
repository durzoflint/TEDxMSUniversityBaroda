About TED

TED is a nonprofit devoted to spreading ideas, usually in the form of short, powerful talks of around 18 minutes. TED began in 1984 as a conference where Technology, Entertainment and Design converged, and today covers almost all topics — from science to business to global issues — in more than 100 languages. It is a global community, welcoming people from every discipline and culture who seek a deeper understanding of the world. We believe passionately in the power of ideas to change attitudes, lives and, ultimately, the world.

About TEDx

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized. (Subject to certain rules and regulations). TEDx events are fully planned and coordinated independently, on a community-by-community basis. The content and design of each TEDx event is unique and developed independently.

RENAISSANCE

Renaissance refers to the Re-birth of ideas. Through TedxTheMSUniversityBaroda we wish that each and every attendee should witness a renaissance of their thoughts. The talks should be thought provoking and open the minds of people to some new ideology or concept. The speakers can talk about ways to look at life from a different perspective, suggest innovative solutions to mundane problems, or talk about that point in their lives when they experienced a renaissance.
