What is TedxTheMSUniversityBaroda?

TED is a nonprofit devoted to spreading ideas, usually in the form of short, powerful talks (18 minutes or less). TED began in 1984 as a conference where Technology, Entertainment and Design converged, and today covers almost all topics from science to business to global issues in more than 100 languages. TEDxTheMSUniversityBaroda is an independently organized licensed TED event organized by the students of M.S.University of Baroda.
